Company Overview

Founded in 2023, Histronomics was created by two (then) high-schoolers with the goal of introducing HS and college history students to the investing field.

Histronomics was founded with an initial $25,000 fund by Josh Zhuang and Henry Watson, and originally had just 8 members. Today, Histronomics has $40,000 in AUM and 30 members, most of whom intend to pursue a career in the historical field but look to investing as a way to apply their historical analysis and explore their interests.
